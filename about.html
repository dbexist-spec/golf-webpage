The Four Majors

1. The Masters

Played every April at Augusta National Golf Club. It is the only major held at the same course every year and is famous for its "Green Jacket" awarded to the winner.

2. The U.S. Open

Known as the hardest test in golf. It is staged at different courses across the USA and is famous for its thick rough and lightning-fast greens that challenge the world's best players.

3. The Open Championship (British Open)

The oldest tournament in the world. It is played on "Links" courses (coastal dunes) in the UK, where players must battle heavy wind and rain to win the Claret Jug.

4. PGA Championship

A high-stakes tournament for the world's elite professionals. It features the largest trophy of the four, known as the Wanamaker Trophy.
